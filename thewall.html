Revolution 01

No Seed Phrase. Ever.

Every traditional crypto wallet gives you 24 random words — your "seed phrase" — and tells you: memorize this, write it on paper, hide it somewhere safe, never lose it. Lose those words and you lose everything. Forever. No recovery. No support. Your life savings — gone.

This has been the single biggest barrier to crypto adoption for ordinary people. Your grandmother cannot use a seed phrase. A farmer in Kerala cannot safely store 24 random English words. A delivery boy on a motorbike cannot protect a piece of paper with his entire financial life written on it.

TheWall eliminates seed phrases entirely. Your wallet is secured by your identity — biometric verification, your Google Authenticator, and in-app confirmation. Three things you already know how to use. Three things that cannot be lost on a piece of paper. Three things that mean: your crypto is yours, forever, with no single point of failure.

Revolution 02

Zero Gas Fees.

In traditional crypto, every single transaction costs gas — a fee paid to the blockchain network to process your transaction. Send ETH to a friend: pay gas. Swap tokens: pay gas. Move assets between wallets: pay gas. Sometimes that gas fee is larger than the amount you're trying to send.

For small-value transactions — exactly the kind that Emobies delivery boys, Eshoppee shop owners, and everyday Kerala families would make — gas fees make crypto completely impractical. Why pay ₹200 in gas to send ₹150 in EmoCoins?

TheWall uses smart account abstraction technology — built using Alchemy's infrastructure — to eliminate gas fees entirely for users. Transactions are bundled, sponsored, and processed without requiring users to hold or spend native tokens for fees. You move your assets. The platform handles the rest. The experience is as simple as sending a WhatsApp message.

Security Architecture

Bank-level protection.
Human-level simplicity.

TheWall uses three independent security layers. All three must be satisfied before any transaction is authorized. Breaking one layer does not compromise your wallet — because the other two remain.

01

Biometric Verification

Your fingerprint or face ID is the first gate. Built into your phone's secure enclave — the most protected storage on any modern device. Even if someone has your phone unlocked, your biometric is theirs alone to provide. No biometric match, no wallet access.

● Active
02

Google Authenticator

The same 2FA system used by Google, Amazon, and every major bank. A time-based one-time password generated on your device, refreshing every 30 seconds. Even if someone knows your password, without this physical device-generated code, they cannot proceed.

● Active
03

In-App Transaction Confirmation

Every transaction requires explicit in-app approval — a deliberate, conscious confirmation that this specific transaction, for this specific amount, to this specific address, is authorized by you right now. No background processing. No silent transfers. Every movement of your assets is visible and intentional.
